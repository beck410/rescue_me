Rescue Me

RescueMe was created to organize the world of dog rescue. Think of it as a database and action center of all the dogs that need to be saved.

There are thousands of organizations around the country doing the great work of saving dogs from shelters and finding families for them to live a happy life. But they run independently and without a link to shelters and to other rescues.

This creates an inefficiency that unfortunately leaves many dogs behind. Thatâ€™s where RescueMe comes in. With RescueMe, anyone can search and find dogs in need of rescue from a shelter, take on foster dogs, and adopt.

How It Works

If you are a rescue organization, log in and create a profile.

  • Shelter Dogs - Click here to see all the dogs currently in shelters around the country.
  • Potential Dogs - Here, you can select a dog from a shelter that you would like to add to your rescue. This is a pending process, you will need to contact the shelter, arrange transport etc.
  • Rescue Dogs - After you selected dogs you want to take into your rescue, and the dog arrives, to keep the site current, make sure you move the dog from Potential to Rescue
  • Snapshot - This is a quick overview of your rescue, stats, dogs available and contact information. You can direct potential fosters, donors, and the general public interested in adopting here.
